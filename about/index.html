Hiya! I’m Kesku, but you can call me Kes.

Who am I?

I’m a tech enthusiast and undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor’s in Data Science & Machine Learning. I’m a self-taught programmer, and I’ve been (badly) coding since I was 9. On the side I moderate OpenAI’s Discord Community, make fun repos on GitHub, and yap all day on Twitter. Oh, and I also write blogs now I guess.

I’m UK-based, balancing my studies with my hobbies and life in general. I’m a huge fan of most things AI/ML, and I’m always looking for new ways to learn and grow in the field. I’m also a huge fan of video games - been playing Minecraft since 2011, and I’ve been hooked on it ever since. I also play a good amount of Yakuza, Forza Horizon, Cyberpunk 2077, and Honkai Star Rail in my free time.

“Kesku” is a nickname I’ve had since I was 15, and it’s stuck with me since then.

It’s actually an acronym:

  • Keep
  • Everything
  • Simple
  • Keep
  • Understanding

When I say “Keep Understanding”, I mean to keep things simple for others to understand. I believe that the best way to learn is to teach, so that’s kind of what I’m doing here. Hopefully, I’m doing this in a way that’s easy to understand - but.. probably not. I’m still learning, after all.

You can find me on Twitter, GitHub, and Discord (kesku). You can also reach out to me via email at admin@kesku.me

What’s this blog about?

Nothing really. Actually, why are you even here? I’m just a student who likes to write about random stuff. I’m not claiming to be a polished writer- far from it. I’m just here to share my experiences learning, my thoughts on the tech industry, and maybe some tutorials here and there. Going over some arxiv papers sounds fun too. I’m not sure yet, but I’m sure I’ll figure it out as I go along. I hope you enjoy your stay here, and if you have any feedback, feel free to reach out to me. I’m always looking to improve.
